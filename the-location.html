The Perth Australia Temple

The Perth Australia Temple, dedicated on May 20, 2001, by President Gordon B. Hinckley, is a sacred LDS Church building in Yokine, Perth, featuring modern architecture with a single spire and white granite construction. It serves as a place for worship and essential spiritual ceremonies, providing a peaceful environment for church members in the region.
